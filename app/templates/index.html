TigerMenus is a convenient to use dining hall menu app for Princeton University with over 100,000 views and about 500 daily active users since its release in March 2017.

It is primarily used by underclassmen undergraduate students who are required to be on the dining hall plan, upperclassmen who want to best use their two free dining hall meals per week, and graduate students who are on meal plans.

It solves the problem of students having to open 6 different web pages (one for each dining hall) whenever they want to check the menus for the upcoming meal.
